Admission Requirements

Documents and criteria required for admission into different levels of education

Primary School (Ages 5-11)

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or passport data page
  • Passport photographs (4 recent copies)
  • Immunization records
  • Report cards from previous school (if applicable)
  • N15,000 non-refundable application fee
  • Successful interview with both parent and child
  • Entrance assessment test for students entering from Primary 2

Junior Secondary School (Ages 11-14)

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or passport data page
  • Passport photographs (4 recent copies)
  • Primary School Leaving Certificate or its equivalent
  • Report cards from last 2 academic years
  • N25,000 non-refundable application fee
  • Successful performance in entrance examination
  • Satisfactory interview with both parent and student

Senior Secondary School (Ages 14-18)

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or passport data page
  • Passport photographs (4 recent copies)
  • Junior WAEC results or its equivalent
  • Report cards from last 3 academic years
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • N30,000 non-refundable application fee
  • Successful performance in entrance examination
  • Satisfactory interview with both parent and student
  • Character reference letter from previous school
